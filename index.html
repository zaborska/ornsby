TO FREEDOM

There’s nothing quite like it. The wind rushing past you. The feel of the road, open and inviting, tantalizing with all of those curvy, windy roads. The sun beating down on you, making you feel alive. You in control of your destiny, following wherever life leads you. Not just any life. A life full of intensity and the indescribable joy of connecting with nature – just you and the bike.

We get that. That’s what we live for. We know that sometimes life isn’t about taking an air conditioned ride to the nearest grocery store. Life is about actually living. Embracing the dirt on your leathers and the gravel under your tyres. Being able to hop on and go, taking a meandering drive for the sheer joy of it. It’s stunning in its simplicity.

At Ornsby Motorcycle Training, we embrace it all. Being able to handle yourself, the powerful machine that propels you into this vigorous adventure, it's more than just safety and assessments. For some, it becomes the only thing that feels right. There's no other way to travel and that's what we love to see. Open the road ahead, it's waiting for you.
